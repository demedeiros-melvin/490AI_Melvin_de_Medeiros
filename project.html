AI Class Project

Project Overview

This project explores the application of artificial intelligence in the context of managerial economics. The goal is to analyze how AI-driven tools can enhance decision-making, reveal market trends, and improve forecasting abilities across modern industries.

It includes data analysis, automation insights, predictive modeling, and a research-driven evaluation of how AI is transforming economic behavior and business strategy.
